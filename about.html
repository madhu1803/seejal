About

Welcome to SEEJAL

We, at SEEJAL are Interior Design Consultants , providing ‘Conceptual Interior Design’ solutions executing ‘Turnkey’ projects using standardized materials & quality products. Our concepts are customized for each client individually -be it Traditional, Contemporary or Transitional. Our designers work on Space Management & deliver designs that best suit your layout, requirements, style & themes… thus no two projects executed by us are identical. Our turnkey projects extend and execution of projects on behalf of Architects and Builders… Asvini Foundations, Ramaiyam Real Estates Private Ltd, Isha homes to name just a few.

With almost two decades of hands on experience having successfully executed more than a hundred projects as Interior Designers ,we offer you only the best in today’s world , using the latest ‘Design Elements’, which besides being modern ,trendy& space saving are economical and easy to maintain. We offer a wide range of designs to suit every budget, besides a unique ‘flexibility’ option of adding on, or upgrading your interiors in future. Our services are of premium quality - a result of our ‘Standard Practice’ of never compromising on our finished products. Contrary to the myth that Conceptual Aesthetic Interiors are not functional, we at SEEJAL deliver interiors with a balance of both aesthetics and functionality. .

Uniqueness in design is a key parameter we follow, for which we coordinate with you on aesthetical and functional aspects related to Interior concepts & designs for your home . The end result being a product that becomes a true reflection of your personality.
